The story of Naruto follows Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja from the Hidden Leaf Village who is feared by his community because a powerful demon, the Nine-Tailed Fox, was sealed within him at birth. Naruto dreams of becoming the village leader, the Hokage, to gain recognition and prove his worth. The series is split into two parts, with the first focusing on his childhood and training alongside teammates Sasuke Uchiha and Sakura Haruno, while the second part explores their growth into powerful ninja, their differing paths, and Naruto's ultimate fight against a dark force that threatens their world.
